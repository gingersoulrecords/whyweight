Your health and safety come first.

During pregnancy or while breastfeeding, it's important to focus on safe, proven strategies for maintaining a healthy weight. We recommend working with your healthcare provider to develop a personalized plan that includes appropriate nutrition and gentle exercise.

Weight management medications are typically not recommended during this time due to potential risks to you and your baby.
